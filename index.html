ProjectKick

ProjectKick was born out of the desire to understand, visualize and connect with
the creative culture of NYC. Kickstarter immediately came to mind as the perfect
gateway, but we didn't want to stop at just one city.

We collected thousands of current or successful project data points, across hundreds
of US cities going as far back as August 2009. We parsed the data to display in three
main ways- by city, category, and funding.

Our goal is to help understand the creative culture across the country and see which
categories the most popular projects fall under and are the most funded.

About

Alina Guzman is a student from New York enrolled in the Digital Marketing program at the Zicklin School of Business at CUNY Baruch College, minoring in Graphic Design. Her hobbies and interests include graphic design, photography, and all things digital media. She is currently the Marketing Coordinator of a Brooklyn based digital media communications firm, Esvee Group. There, she takes the lead in design and content marketing. Her previous work experience includes a Social Media Coordinator/ Event Photographer position at MatchPuppy, a social networking start up for dog owners. Alina's skills include Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, and Dreamweaver. Alina started editing images for fun long before her interest evolved into graphic and web design. Her dedication had motivated her to take the position of President of the Baruch Photography Club in 2010, where she lead tutorials in Photoshop and excursions around the city everywhere from abandoned tunnels to graffitied parks. To this day, she continues her hobby, by capturing all that is picturesque to her eyes, with a focus on New York street life.
